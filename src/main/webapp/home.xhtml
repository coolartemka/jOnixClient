jOnix Platform

This is the client, in which you can get access to the messages which are intended to your organization. You can send new messages, or browse the ones that have been sent to you.
jOnix platform provides the following features:
  • access to the messages other publishers have sent to you
  • browsing particular products
  • receiving messages from other publishers as soon as they notify you about it
  • message sending utilities
  • seeing the history of changes in one particular product
  • etc.
